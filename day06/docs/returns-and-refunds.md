# Returns and Refunds Policy

Customers can request returns for most products within the return window.

## Return windows
- Standard products: 7 days from delivery.
- Premium products: 14 days from delivery.
- Non-returnable categories: personal-care items and software licenses.

## Eligibility rules
- Product must be unused and in original packaging.
- All accessories and invoice must be included.
- The return reason must be selected at request time.

## Refund timelines
- UPI/wallet refunds: 2 to 4 business days.
- Card refunds: 5 to 7 business days.
- Net banking: 3 to 5 business days.

## Important note
If a returned item fails quality check, the refund is rejected and the item is shipped back.
