# Product Warranty Policy

We provide warranty coverage for eligible products sold through the eComBot catalog.

## Standard coverage
- Electronics: 12 months manufacturer warranty.
- Accessories: 6 months limited warranty.
- Refurbished items: 3 months seller warranty.

## What is covered
- Manufacturing defects.
- Hardware failure under normal use.
- Faulty components at delivery.

## What is not covered
- Physical damage, water damage, or misuse.
- Unauthorized repairs or tampering.
- Consumables such as batteries after normal wear.

## Claim process
1. Share the order ID and product serial number.
2. Upload issue photos or a short video.
3. Support validates the claim within 2 business days.
4. Eligible claims are repaired, replaced, or refunded.
