# Delivery and Shipping SLA

This document describes shipping timelines and service levels.

## Delivery speed
- Standard shipping: 3 to 5 business days.
- Express shipping: 1 to 2 business days.
- Same-day delivery: available in selected city zones.

## Delays
Delivery may be delayed due to severe weather, strikes, or address verification issues.

## Tracking updates
- Tracking starts once the package is handed to the courier.
- Status transitions include Packed, Shipped, Out for Delivery, and Delivered.

## Failed delivery attempts
- Courier attempts delivery up to 3 times.
- After 3 failed attempts, the order is returned to origin.

## Address changes
Address changes are allowed only before the order reaches Shipped status.
